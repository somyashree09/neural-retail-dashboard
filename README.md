## 🔍 Project Overview
Retail businesses generate enormous volumes of data daily — but raw data alone doesn't drive decisions. **NeuralRetail** transforms retail transaction data into actionable business intelligence through a unified, interactive dashboard ecosystem.

Built on the **Global E-Commerce Sales dataset**, the platform addresses six core business challenges:

- Difficulty monitoring sales performance across products, regions, and time periods
- Limited visibility into customer purchasing behavior and retention risks
- Challenges in identifying profitable vs. underperforming products
- Inventory stockouts and dead stock accumulation
- Lack of forecasting capabilities for strategic planning
- No centralized view of regional business performance

## 🧠 Key Business Insights

**Sales:** Q4 generated the strongest performance with $136,145 in revenue — 33% higher than Q1 ($102,251). October was the single highest-performing month at $51.1K.

**Products:** Furniture drove $256K in revenue, followed by Technology at $140K. A small product group accounted for the majority of profits — classic Pareto distribution confirmed.

**Customers:** 1,534 customers with an average lifetime value of $316 and a repeat purchase rate of 25.6%. Consumer segment was the largest (864 customers).

**Regional:** Europe was the top-performing region ($137,006 revenue, $45,672 profit). Mexico was the highest revenue country at $47,217.

**Inventory:** 21 dead stock products and 3 critical SKUs identified, with 35 products requiring reorder — enabling proactive inventory planning.

**Churn:** LSTM model achieved AUC-ROC of 1.00 with MAPE of 9.4%. 1,413 high-risk customers flagged for retention intervention.
